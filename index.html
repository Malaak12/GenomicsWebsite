Welcome to Genomics and Proteomics in Drug Discovery!

This website is dedicated to our project on Genomics and Proteomics in Drug Discovery using ChatGPT-4.

About the Project

Our project aims to explore the application of genomics and proteomics in drug discovery. Leveraging the power of ChatGPT-4, we are developing a system that can assist researchers in analyzing genomic and proteomic data to discover potential drug candidates.
